Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are our priority

Effective Date: November 19, 2024

Last Updated: November 19, 2024

Omoola Pharmacy & Stores ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our website and services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services to you:

1.1 Personal Information

Information that identifies you as an individual, including:

  • Contact Information: Full name, email address, phone number, delivery address, billing address
  • Account Information: Username, password (encrypted), account preferences, profile picture (optional)
  • Identification: Date of birth, government-issued ID numbers (for prescription verification)
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, bank account information, billing address (processed through secure payment gateways)

1.2 Health Information

Sensitive health-related information protected under Nigerian data protection and healthcare privacy laws:

  • Prescription Information: Prescription details, prescribing doctor information, medication history
  • Medical History: Allergies, medical conditions, previous medications (when provided by you)
  • Health Inquiries: Questions asked to pharmacists, health consultations
  • Purchase History: Medications and health products purchased

1.3 Technical Information

Information automatically collected when you use our website:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked, search queries
  • Cookies and Tracking: Cookies, web beacons, analytics data (see Cookie Policy below)
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address (with your consent for precise location)

1.4 Communications

  • Emails, messages, and calls with customer service
  • Survey responses and feedback
  • Newsletter subscriptions and marketing preferences
  • Social media interactions

2. How We Collect Information

2.1 Direct Collection

  • When you create an account on our website
  • When you place an order or make a purchase
  • When you upload a prescription
  • When you contact customer service
  • When you subscribe to our newsletter
  • When you participate in surveys or promotions
  • When you interact with our pharmacists

2.2 Automatic Collection

  • Through cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Through web analytics tools (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Through server logs and access records
  • Through mobile application usage (if applicable)

2.3 Third-Party Sources

  • Healthcare providers (for prescription verification)
  • Payment processors
  • Delivery partners
  • Social media platforms (if you sign in through social media)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

3.1 Service Provision

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Verify and dispense prescription medications safely
  • Provide customer service and support
  • Manage your account and preferences
  • Facilitate payments and prevent fraud
  • Arrange delivery of products

3.2 Communication

  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • Provide prescription refill reminders
  • Respond to inquiries and requests
  • Send important service announcements
  • Provide health information and medication guidance

3.3 Marketing (with your consent)

  • Send promotional offers and special deals
  • Share health tips and wellness content
  • Inform you about new products and services
  • Conduct market research and surveys

3.4 Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyze website usage and improve user experience
  • Develop new products and services
  • Conduct research and analytics
  • Test new features and functionality

3.5 Legal and Security

  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Enforce our Terms and Conditions
  • Resolve disputes and troubleshoot problems
  • Maintain records as required by pharmaceutical regulations

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our business:

  • Payment Processors: To process your payments securely
  • Delivery Partners: To deliver your orders
  • Cloud Storage: To store data securely
  • Email Services: To send communications
  • Analytics Providers: To understand website usage
  • IT Support: To maintain our systems

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

4.2 Healthcare Providers

  • Your prescribing doctor (for prescription verification)
  • Other healthcare professionals (with your explicit consent)
  • Healthcare facilities (when medically necessary)

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law:

  • To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or subpoenas
  • To regulatory authorities (PCN, NAFDAC, etc.)
  • To law enforcement agencies in case of suspected illegal activity
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others
  • In connection with fraud prevention and investigation

4.4 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified of any such change.

4.5 With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you have given us explicit consent to do so.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

5.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure Servers: Data stored on secure, encrypted servers
  • Firewalls: Network firewalls to prevent unauthorized access
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication
  • Regular Security Audits: Periodic security assessments and penetration testing

5.2 Organizational Safeguards

  • Staff Training: All employees trained on data protection and privacy
  • Confidentiality Agreements: Staff bound by confidentiality obligations
  • Limited Access: Access to personal data restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Data Protection Officer: Dedicated personnel responsible for privacy compliance

5.3 Payment Security

  • PCI DSS compliant payment processing
  • Credit card information not stored on our servers
  • Secure payment gateways with tokenization

Important: While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your information to the best of our ability.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

6.1 Access and Portability

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Request transfer of your data to another service provider

6.2 Correction and Update

  • Update your personal information through your account settings
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Update your communication preferences

6.3 Deletion

  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Close your account and delete associated data

Note: We may be required to retain certain information for legal, regulatory, or legitimate business purposes (e.g., prescription records must be kept for 5 years as per Nigerian pharmacy regulations).

6.4 Objection and Restriction

  • Object to processing of your personal information for certain purposes
  • Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

6.5 Withdraw Consent

  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Note: Withdrawal may affect your ability to use certain services

How to Exercise Your Rights

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

7.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience and understand how you use our site.

7.2 Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies (Always Active)

  • Enable basic website functionality
  • Maintain your shopping cart
  • Keep you logged in
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

Performance Cookies

  • Collect anonymous usage statistics
  • Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Examples: Google Analytics

Functionality Cookies

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Personalize your experience
  • Provide enhanced features

Marketing Cookies

  • Track your browsing habits
  • Show relevant advertisements
  • Measure advertising effectiveness
  • Requires your consent

7.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies
  • Cookie Consent Tool: Use our cookie preference center
  • Opt-Out Links: Opt out of third-party advertising cookies

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete such information.

Purchases for minors must be made by a parent or legal guardian.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Retention Periods

  • Account Information: Until account closure plus 1 year
  • Prescription Records: Minimum 5 years (as required by law)
  • Purchase History: 7 years for tax and accounting purposes
  • Marketing Data: Until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • CCTV Footage (if applicable): 30 days unless required for investigation

After the retention period expires, we securely delete or anonymize your information.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information is primarily stored and processed in Nigeria. If we transfer data outside Nigeria (e.g., to cloud storage providers or service providers in other countries), we ensure adequate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Adequacy decisions by regulatory authorities
  • Certified data protection frameworks

11. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, social media platforms, or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

12. Marketing Communications

12.1 How We Contact You

With your consent, we may send you:

  • Email newsletters with health tips and wellness advice
  • Promotional offers and special discounts
  • Product recommendations based on your interests
  • SMS notifications about orders and promotions

12.2 Opting Out

You can unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time by:

  • Clicking "Unsubscribe" in any marketing email
  • Replying "STOP" to marketing SMS messages
  • Updating preferences in your account settings
  • Contacting customer service

Note: Even if you opt out of marketing emails, we may still send you important service-related communications (order confirmations, shipping updates, etc.).

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or for other reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website with a new "Effective Date"
  • Sending an email notification to registered users
  • Displaying a prominent notice on our website

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
