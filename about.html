About Omolola Pharmacy & Stores

Your trusted healthcare partner since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Omolola Pharmacy & Stores began with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide accessible, reliable healthcare to our community. What started as a small neighborhood pharmacy has grown into a trusted healthcare destination, serving thousands of families across Nigeria.

Our founder, Mrs. Omolola Adeyemi, a licensed pharmacist with over 20 years of experience, envisioned a pharmacy that goes beyond dispensing medications. She wanted to create a place where people feel cared for, where their health concerns are heard, and where they receive not just medicines, but comprehensive health guidance.

Today, we combine traditional pharmaceutical expertise with modern e-commerce convenience, making quality healthcare accessible to everyone, everywhere.
