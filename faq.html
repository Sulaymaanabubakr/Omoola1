General Questions

Omoola Pharmacy & Stores is a fully licensed and accredited pharmacy and supermarket serving customers across Nigeria since 2015. We provide prescription medications, over-the-counter medicines, health supplements, and everyday groceries with professional care and convenient delivery options. Our team of licensed pharmacists ensures every product meets the highest quality standards.

Yes, absolutely. We are fully licensed by the Pharmacists Council of Nigeria (PCN License: PCN-12345) and all our products are approved by NAFDAC (National Agency for Food and Drug Administration and Control). Our pharmacy is operated by qualified, registered pharmacists who adhere to strict pharmaceutical standards and ethical guidelines.

Our physical store is open Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays. However, our online store is available 24/7, and you can place orders at any time. Orders placed after business hours will be processed on the next working day. For urgent after-hours inquiries, you can reach our emergency hotline at +234 800 000 0000.

Products & Availability

All products on our website display real-time stock availability. If a product is in stock, you'll see "In Stock" or the available quantity displayed on the product page. If an item is temporarily unavailable, it will be marked as "Out of Stock." You can sign up for email notifications to be alerted when out-of-stock items become available again. For prescription medications, please call us to confirm availability before placing your order.

Yes, you can order prescription medications through our website. You'll need to upload a clear photo or scanned copy of your valid prescription during checkout. Our licensed pharmacists will review your prescription before dispensing the medication. For controlled substances and certain medications, we may need to verify the prescription directly with your doctor. If you have questions about your prescription, our pharmacists are available for consultation.

Yes, we stock a wide range of generic medications that are equally effective as brand-name versions but typically more affordable. Generic medications contain the same active ingredients and meet the same quality standards as their brand-name counterparts. Our pharmacists can advise you on available generic alternatives for your prescription. If your doctor has specified "no substitution" on your prescription, we will dispense only the brand prescribed.

If you can't find a specific product on our website, please contact us via phone (+234 800 000 0000), email (info@omoola.com), or through our contact form. We may be able to special order the product for you or suggest suitable alternatives. Many specialty medications and uncommon products can be sourced within 2-5 business days. Our team will provide you with availability and pricing information promptly.

Orders & Payments

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience: Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve), Bank Transfers, USSD, Mobile Money (MTN, Airtel, Glo, 9mobile), and Cash on Delivery (available in select areas). All online payments are processed through secure, encrypted payment gateways. For Cash on Delivery, payment is collected by our delivery personnel upon receipt of your order.

Yes, absolutely. Your payment security is our top priority. Our website uses 256-bit SSL encryption to protect your personal and financial information. We comply with PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards) and do not store your complete card details on our servers. All transactions are processed through trusted, certified payment gateways that meet international security standards.

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive an order confirmation email with your order number. When your order is dispatched, we'll send you a tracking link via SMS and email. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website and viewing your order history. For real-time updates, you can call our customer service team with your order number.

You can modify or cancel your order within 30 minutes of placing it by contacting our customer service immediately at +234 800 000 0000 or info@omoola.com. Once an order has been processed and dispatched, it cannot be cancelled, but you can return it according to our returns policy. Please note that prescription orders may be processed quickly and might not be eligible for cancellation once the pharmacist has begun preparing them.

Returns & Refunds

We accept returns on eligible items within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unopened, unused, and in their original packaging. For health and safety reasons, prescription medications, opened medicines, perishable items, and personal care products cannot be returned once delivered. Full details are available in our Returns Policy. To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for return.

Once we receive your returned item and verify it meets our return conditions, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The refund will be credited to your original payment method. Bank transfers may take an additional 3-5 business days to reflect in your account depending on your bank. You'll receive an email confirmation once your refund has been processed.

We sincerely apologize if this happens. Please contact us immediately within 24 hours of receiving your order at +234 800 000 0000 or info@omoola.com. Provide photos of the damaged item or the incorrect product received. We'll arrange for a free pickup and send you the correct item or provide a full refund at no additional cost to you. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we'll resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
