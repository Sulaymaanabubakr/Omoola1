Returns Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority

FAQ Shipping Info Returns Policy Privacy Policy Terms & Conditions

Effective Date: November 19, 2024

At Omoola Pharmacy & Stores, your satisfaction and safety are our top priorities. We understand that sometimes products may not meet your expectations or arrive damaged. This Returns Policy outlines the conditions under which products can be returned and how we process refunds and exchanges.

Please read this policy carefully. Due to the nature of pharmaceutical and healthcare products, some items cannot be returned for health, safety, and regulatory reasons.

Return Period

You have 14 calendar days from the date of delivery to return eligible items for a refund or exchange. Returns must be initiated within this timeframe by contacting our customer service team.

Important: The 14-day period begins on the day you receive your order, not the day you place it. Weekend days and public holidays are included in this count.

Returnable Items

The following items can be returned if they meet our return conditions:

  • Non-prescription medicines: Unopened, sealed, with original packaging intact
  • Health supplements and vitamins: Unopened, sealed, with tamper-evident seal intact
  • Medical devices: Unopened, unused, in original packaging
  • Personal care products: Unopened, sealed, with packaging intact
  • Groceries (non-perishable): Unopened, undamaged, within expiry date
  • Beauty and cosmetic products: Unopened, sealed, in original condition
  • Home health equipment: Unused, in original packaging with all accessories

Non-Returnable Items

For health, safety, and regulatory reasons, the following items cannot be returned once delivered:

‚ùå Items That Cannot Be Returned

  • Prescription medications: All prescription drugs, regardless of condition, cannot be returned once dispensed
  • Opened medicines: Any medication or health product with broken seal or packaging
  • Controlled substances: Due to strict regulatory requirements
  • Perishable items: Fresh produce, dairy products, frozen foods, and items with short shelf life
  • Personal hygiene products: Opened items such as toothbrushes, razors, thermometers (unless defective)
  • Intimate care products: For hygiene and safety reasons
  • Baby formula and food: Once seal is broken or packaging is opened
  • Special order items: Products specially ordered for you that are not part of regular stock
  • Clearance or sale items: Products marked as "final sale" or "clearance"
  • Gift cards and vouchers: Non-refundable once purchased
  • Compounded medications: Custom-made formulations prepared specifically for you

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, items must meet ALL of the following conditions:

  1. Unopened and Unused: Products must be in their original, unopened condition with all seals intact
  2. Original Packaging: Items must be in their original packaging, including boxes, bottles, instructions, and any accessories
  3. Proof of Purchase: You must provide your order number, receipt, or invoice
  4. Within Return Period: Return must be initiated within 14 days of delivery
  5. Not Expired: Products must have at least 3 months until expiry date
  6. Saleable Condition: Items must be in a condition that allows us to resell them safely
  7. Original Payment Method Verifiable: For refunds to original payment method

Note: Items that don't meet these conditions will not be accepted for return. We reserve the right to refuse returns that don't comply with this policy.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive damaged, defective, or incorrect items, we will gladly replace or refund them at no cost to you.

What to Do If Your Item Arrives Damaged

  1. Inspect Upon Delivery: Check your order as soon as it arrives
  2. Document the Damage: Take clear photos of:
    • The outer packaging showing damage
    • The damaged product
    • Any shipping labels or tracking information
  3. Contact Us Immediately: Report the issue within 24 hours of delivery by:
    • Phone: +234 800 000 0000
    • Email: info@omoola.com
    • Through your online account
  4. Do Not Dispose: Keep the damaged item and all packaging until we resolve the issue

What We'll Do

  • Verify the damage claim (may request photos)
  • Arrange FREE pickup of the damaged item
  • Send a replacement item immediately (if in stock)
  • Process a full refund if replacement isn't desired or available
  • Cover all associated costs (return shipping, replacement delivery)

Response Time: We aim to resolve damaged item claims within 24-48 hours of notification.

Wrong Item Delivered

If you receive an incorrect item:

  1. Contact us immediately at +234 800 000 0000 or info@omoola.com
  2. Provide your order number and description of the incorrect item
  3. Send photos of the item you received
  4. Keep the item in its original condition

We will:

  • Arrange FREE pickup of the incorrect item
  • Ship the correct item immediately at no extra charge
  • Provide a full refund if you no longer want the item
  • Offer a discount or store credit for the inconvenience

How to Initiate a Return

Step-by-Step Return Process

Step 1: Contact Customer Service

Reach out to us through any of these channels:

  • Phone: +234 800 000 0000 (Mon-Sat, 8am-8pm)
  • Email: info@omoola.com
  • Online: Log into your account and go to "My Orders" ‚Üí Select order ‚Üí "Request Return"
  • In Store: Visit us at 3, Isaga Bus Stop, Opposite The Gem International School, Owode Yewa, Ogun State

Step 2: Provide Required Information

Have the following ready:

  • Order number or receipt
  • Item(s) you wish to return
  • Reason for return
  • Photos (if item is damaged or defective)
  • Preferred resolution (refund, exchange, or store credit)

Step 3: Return Authorization

Our team will:

  • Review your return request
  • Verify the item is eligible for return
  • Issue a Return Authorization Number (RAN)
  • Provide return instructions and shipping label (if applicable)

Important: Returns without a Return Authorization Number may not be accepted.

Step 4: Package the Item

  • Place item in its original packaging
  • Include all accessories, manuals, and bonus items
  • Write your RAN clearly on the package
  • Seal the package securely

Step 5: Return the Item

You have two options:

  • Pickup Service: We can arrange to collect the item from your address (fees may apply for customer-initiated returns)
  • Drop-off: Bring the item to our store during business hours

Step 6: Inspection & Processing

  • We inspect the returned item within 2-3 business days
  • Verify it meets return conditions
  • Process your refund, exchange, or store credit
  • Send you confirmation via email

Refund Options

1. Full Refund to Original Payment Method

  • Refund is credited to the payment method used for purchase
  • Processing time: 5-7 business days after item is received and inspected
  • Bank transfers may take additional 3-5 days to reflect in your account
  • Original shipping fees are non-refundable (except for damaged/defective items)

2. Exchange for Different Item

  • Choose a different product of equal or lesser value
  • If new item costs more, pay the difference
  • If new item costs less, difference refunded or issued as store credit
  • New item shipped within 2-3 business days

3. Store Credit

  • Receive full refund amount as store credit
  • Store credit has no expiry date
  • Can be used for any future purchase
  • Transferable to family members if desired
  • Issued immediately upon return approval

Partial Returns

If you ordered multiple items and only want to return some of them:

  • You can return individual items from your order
  • Each item must meet return conditions independently
  • Partial refund will be issued for returned items only
  • If partial return brings order below free shipping threshold, shipping costs may be deducted from refund

Gifts & Gift Recipients

If you received an item as a gift and wish to return it:

  • Contact us with the order number (if known) or gift receipt
  • Returns are processed as store credit since you didn't make the original payment
  • Gift giver will not be notified of the return
  • All other return conditions apply

Product Recalls

In the event of a product recall:

  • We will contact all affected customers immediately via email, SMS, and phone
  • Provide clear instructions on return or disposal
  • Arrange FREE pickup or accept in-store returns
  • Issue full refund including shipping costs
  • Offer alternative products if available
  • Provide safety information and guidance

If you believe you have a recalled product, stop using it immediately and contact us at +234 800 000 0000.

Prescription Medication Policy

Important Notice: Due to legal and safety regulations, prescription medications cannot be returned once dispensed by our pharmacists. This is because:

  • We cannot verify the medication's integrity after it leaves our pharmacy
  • Regulatory guidelines prohibit re-dispensing returned medications
  • Patient safety is paramount and cannot be compromised
  • Chain of custody cannot be maintained

However, we will issue refunds for prescription medications if:

  • The medication was incorrectly dispensed due to our error
  • The product is damaged or defective
  • The medication is past its expiry date when received
  • You can provide proof that the medication was not collected or opened

In such cases, the medication must be returned to our pharmacy for proper disposal according to regulatory guidelines. Refunds will be processed after verification.
