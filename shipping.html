Order Processing & Dispatch

How We Process Your Order

  1. Order Confirmation: Once you complete your payment, you'll receive an order confirmation email with your order number and estimated delivery date.
  2. Prescription Verification (if applicable): For prescription medications, our licensed pharmacists will review and verify your prescription. This typically takes 1-2 hours during business hours.
  3. Order Preparation: Our team carefully picks and packs your items, ensuring proper packaging for safe transit, especially for fragile items and medications requiring specific storage conditions.
  4. Quality Check: Every order undergoes a final quality check to ensure accuracy and product condition.
  5. Dispatch: Your order is handed over to our delivery partner, and you'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email.

Order Processing Times

  • Regular orders: Processed within 24 hours
  • Prescription orders: Processed within 2-4 hours during business hours (may take longer if prescription verification is required)
  • Large orders (10+ items): May require up to 48 hours for processing
  • Weekend orders: Orders placed on Saturday after 2 PM or on Sunday will be processed on Monday
  • Public holidays: No processing; orders will be handled on the next business day

Delivery for Prescription Medications

Prescription medications require special handling to ensure safety, compliance, and proper patient care:

What You Need to Provide

  • Valid prescription from a licensed healthcare provider
  • Clear photo or scanned copy of the prescription uploaded during checkout
  • Valid identification (driver's license, national ID, or international passport)
  • Contact information for your prescribing doctor (for verification if needed)

Pharmacist Review Process

  • Our licensed pharmacists review every prescription before dispensing
  • We may contact your doctor to verify the prescription or clarify dosage
  • If there are concerns about drug interactions or contraindications, our pharmacist will contact you
  • Prescriptions are kept on file for legal and medical record purposes

Controlled Substances

For controlled medications (e.g., certain pain medications, sedatives, stimulants):

  • Original prescription may be required (not a photocopy)
  • Additional verification steps may apply
  • Delivery must be signed for by the patient or authorized representative
  • Valid photo ID must be presented upon delivery

Medication Storage During Transit

  • Medications requiring refrigeration are transported in insulated cool boxes
  • Temperature-sensitive items include appropriate cold packs
  • All medications are protected from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures

Receiving Your Delivery

What to Expect

  • Our delivery personnel will arrive within the estimated delivery window
  • You'll receive a call or SMS 30 minutes before arrival
  • Delivery staff are professional, uniformed, and carry company identification
  • For security, always verify the identity of the delivery person

Delivery Acceptance

  1. Inspect the package for any visible damage
  2. Check that the number of items matches your order
  3. For Cash on Delivery, make payment to the delivery person and receive your receipt
  4. Sign the delivery confirmation
  5. If you notice any issues, document them with photos before signing

If You're Not Home

  • Our delivery person will attempt to contact you by phone
  • You can arrange for someone else to receive the delivery on your behalf (they'll need a copy of your order confirmation and valid ID)
  • If no one is available, we'll leave a notice and contact you to reschedule delivery
  • Redelivery is free for the first attempt; subsequent redelivery attempts may incur fees

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect:

  • Do not accept the delivery if the packaging is severely damaged
  • If you notice damage after accepting, contact us immediately at +234 800 000 0000
  • Take photos of the damaged items and packaging
  • We'll arrange for a replacement or refund within 24-48 hours
  • Return shipping for damaged/incorrect items is FREE

Delivery of Perishable Items & Groceries

For grocery and perishable items, we take extra care to ensure freshness:

  • Perishable items are packed in insulated bags or cool boxes
  • Ice packs are included for items requiring cold storage
  • We prioritize delivery of orders containing perishables
  • Recommended delivery option: Same-day or express delivery for maximum freshness
  • Please inspect perishable items immediately upon delivery

Important: For quality and safety reasons, perishable items cannot be returned once delivered. Please check your order carefully upon receipt.

International Shipping

Currently, we only deliver within Nigeria. We are working on expanding our services to international customers in the near future. If you're outside Nigeria and need our products, please contact us at info@omoola.com, and we'll do our best to assist you.

Delivery Delays

While we strive for timely delivery, occasional delays may occur due to:

  • Adverse weather conditions
  • Traffic congestion in urban areas
  • Public holidays and festive periods
  • Incomplete or incorrect delivery addresses
  • Restricted access areas requiring special clearance
  • Logistics partner challenges

If your order is significantly delayed beyond the estimated delivery date, please contact us, and we'll investigate immediately and provide you with updates.

Shipping Restrictions

Certain items cannot be shipped or have shipping restrictions:

Cannot Be Shipped

  • Controlled substances without valid prescription and verification
  • Expired or recalled products
  • Counterfeit or unauthorized items
  • Hazardous materials (unless properly licensed and packaged)

Restricted Items (Special Handling Required)

  • Refrigerated medications and biologics
  • High-value items (may require signature and ID verification)
  • Controlled drugs (additional verification and documentation required)
  • Large or bulky items (may incur additional shipping fees)
